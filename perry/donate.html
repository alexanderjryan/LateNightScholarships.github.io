Donate

The Late Night Scholarships fund is 100% financed by small donors. The fund's donorbase is composed primarily of Perry alumni with 100% of all donations going to the scholarship awards for the scholarship winners. All donors will have the option to review the anonymized applications and vote for which scholarship applicants should receive the awards. The applicants with the most votes receive the scholarships and are announced at Perry's annual Scholarship Award Ceremony.

Donate Using PayPal

The primary mode of donating to the scholarship fund is through our PayPal page. You can access this page here.

Donate Using Venmo

The secondary mode of donating to the scholarship fund is through Venmo. Please contact us using the information presented on the "Contact" page to request the venmo account name.

Donate by Sending a Check

The final mode of donating to the scholarship fund is by sending a check. Please contact us using the information presented on the "Contact" page to request the mailing address.
