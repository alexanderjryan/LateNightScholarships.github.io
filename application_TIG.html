15 for 15 Scholarship Application

The 15 for 15 Scholarship Fund was created by David Lee Morgan Jr. (author of The Massillon Tigers: 15 for 15 book), Scott Ryan (Section 14 of Paul Brown Tiger Stadium), and David Bushman (cofounder of Fayetteville Mafia Press) to offer two $500 scholarships to graduating Massillon students in the class of 2021. One scholarship will be awarded to a male, and one scholarship will be awarded to a female. To apply for the 15 for 15 scholarship, please fill out the following application form in its entirety. The deadline for completing the scholarship application will be April 1, 2021. The winner of the scholarship will be announced in May 2021.
